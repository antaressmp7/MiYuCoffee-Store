Our Story

About Mi;Yu

Mi;Yu was born from a simple belief — that the best coffee is shared. Every cup we craft begins with carefully sourced beans, roasted in small batches to bring out their most honest flavors.

The semicolon in our name is intentional. It's a pause — a breath between two people, two flavors, two moments. Our brew is where Mi meets Yu.

🌿
Our Values

We believe in slow mornings, sustainable sourcing, and spaces that feel like a second home. Whether you're here with someone or finding yourself — Mi;Yu holds space for both.

From our latte art classes to late-night jazz nights, every corner of our store is crafted for connection.
